About me

I was born and raised in Roma, Gotland, with my five siblings and parents. After high school, I moved to Stockholm to study at KTH, where I found both system development and entrepreneurship to be very interesting.

Being able to grow both personally and professionaly is important to me, which I feel I can do by problem solving and innovation. From January 2016, I work full time with my own business Lingo AB and Oak where the most time is spent on software development and architecture but also with our business models, marketing and product development.

I am driven by designing software systems in such a way that they are easy to understand and maintainable. My belief is that that can be achieved by making use of good programming practices, such as SOLID, TDD and Clean code. I like to both go in the details in a system by doing actual programming, and also designing and thinking about the architecture.

Other major interests are music, sports, gardening, cooking and photography.
